What is Outyard?

Outyard is an independently organized hackathon at Uppsala University. The hackathon is meant to inspire personal programming projects and is an excellent way to learn programming.

We always look for more people to join us. We recommend you have moderate programming experience, for example knowing how to draw things to the screen.

Join us on Discord, build something awesome and get notified of future hackathons!

Outyard is organized by Carl Enlund (carl@crl.io)
